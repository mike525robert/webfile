Eligibility Criteria

  • Registered businesses
  • Turnover: From £500k

  • Over 50% of turnover from trading activity (e.g. not from investments)
  • Facility primarily for trading activity

Documents Required

You’ll need to email the following:

  • 2019 Year-End Profit and Loss statement
  • 2019 Year-End Balance Sheet
  • Year-to-date Profit and Loss statement
  • Year-to-date Balance sheet
  • Last 3 months business bank statements
  • Cash flow or profit-and-loss forecast for the next 18 months or ideally next 36 months

Information

  • Accepted file types include .pdf, .docx, .xlsx, .csv
